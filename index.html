Your agents

How the system moves work.

Each agent has a role, a set of sub-functions, and a fixed handoff rule. Work flows between them without you routing it. That is what makes it a system, not a list of assistants.

07

Executive · CEO

Sets direction. Handles escalations. Any decision that cannot be undone routes here first. Not involved in routine production, but every cycle starts and resets with this one. The reason the other six have a north star.

01

Communications · CCO

Inbound triage, outbound drafting, internal routing. First and last contact in the cycle. Escalates directly to CEO when a message carries reputational or legal risk. Nothing goes unanswered.

02

Marketing · CMO

Copy, content, campaigns, audience growth. Produces everything published under your name. Every piece routes directly to Operations for review before it ships. Never self-approves.

03

Finance · CFO

Revenue tracking, spend optimization, risk assessment, pricing validation. Sales cannot commit to a number without this agent's sign-off. Keeps the engine solvent and the deals structured correctly.

04

Technology · CTO

Briefs to builds, specs to deliverables, system architecture. Every completed build routes to Operations for sign-off before going live. Judged by what ships, not what was planned.

05

Operations · COO

Quality review, approval, rejection and correction. Receives work from Marketing and Technology. Approves or sends it back with a specific fix. The single checkpoint everything passes through. Nothing ships without this one.

06

Sales · CSO

Pipeline management, deal closing, client relationships, follow-up cadence. Every price and deal structure is validated by Finance before Sales commits. Revenue lands here and the cycle resets.

The handoff rules

Marketing produces, Operations reviews. Technology builds, Operations signs off. Sales prices, Finance validates first. Communications escalates to CEO on any risk. Rejections go back directly to the agent who produced the work, with a correction attached. CEO only enters on escalations and irreversible calls. Every other handoff runs autonomously. No gaps, no guessing, no routing through you.
